Dodge Charger Overview

The Dodge Charger is a full-size muscle car that has been in production since 1966. Known for its aggressive styling and powerful performance, the Charger has earned a legendary status in automotive history. It offers a range of engine options, from V6 to HEMI V8 engines, delivering impressive horsepower and torque. The Charger is celebrated for its bold, muscular exterior design, characterized by a wide stance, sharp lines, and distinctive front grille. Inside, it features a spacious cabin with modern technology and comfort. With rear-wheel drive and available all-wheel drive, it offers a thrilling driving experience. The Charger also boasts a reputation for being a family-friendly performance car, combining power with practicality. Over the years, it has been featured in countless movies, TV shows, and video games, cementing its place in pop culture. It continues to evolve with each generation, balancing classic muscle car appeal with contemporary innovations. The Dodge Charger remains one of the most iconic American cars of all time.
