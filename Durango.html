Dodge Durango Overview

The **Dodge Durango** is a midsize SUV that blends muscle-car performance with family-friendly versatility. Its bold exterior features a striking grille, LED headlights, and an athletic stance, while the interior offers three rows of seating for up to seven passengers and advanced tech like the Uconnect infotainment system. Powertrain options range from a 3.6L V6 with 295 horsepower to a supercharged 6.2L V8 in the SRT Hellcat, delivering 710 horsepower and a best-in-class towing capacity of 8,700 pounds. The Durango SRT trims include features like launch control and Brembo brakes for track-ready performance. Safety features include adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and blind-spot monitoring. Whether you need a family SUV or a high-performance powerhouse, the Durango’s blend of practicality, power, and aggressive styling sets it apart. It’s an SUV that effortlessly combines utility with Dodge’s signature performance.
